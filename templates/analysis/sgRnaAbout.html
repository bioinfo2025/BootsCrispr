My organism’s genome isn’t available on BootsCrispr. How do I add it?


A:You can contact us through the Contact module and leave your email, and we will initialize the species data for you.

If the experimental results are lost, how can they be retrieved?


A:You only need to provide us with your task token through the Contact module and leave your email. After receiving your information, we will send you the relevant tasks.

What should I do if I want to execute tasks in bulk?


A:Both the sgRNA Design and Search Off Targets modules support batch task execution. The "sgRNA Design" module selects "Using gene sequence" or "Using fasta file" with ">" as the interval between sequences. The 'Search Off Targets' module uses'>' as the interval when inputting sequences.

What should I do if an error occurs during the data analysis process?


A:You provide your analysis related data, and we will solve the related errors for you.

Any other queries ?


A:If you encounter any issues during the use of BootsCrispr, you can contact us through the Contact module. As long as you provide your email, we will solve them for you upon receipt.
